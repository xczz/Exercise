 Email address book includes two types of email address:*General address, include information:email address (unique), name, phone number*Group address, include information:email address (unique), name, list of address(each address should be a general address).Write an Email address book management program with C/C++ language:*Give an email address, find its information. If given address is a group address, should list all the general addresses in this group.*Add/Remove one email address.*Read email address book from a source file.*Save email address book to a destination file.
